Features

What can QR Codes do for you?

QR COdes have become quite popular as smartphones became instrumental to code scanning.

They have been employed in a number of beneficial ways, and QReate is here to help you find how they can be useful to you.

Most used QR code features

Payments

In a world that is going cashless, QR Codes have proven to be very effective. Embed payment links into your code and access them with a quick scan.

View Documents

QR Codes are very helpful with minimalistic documentation. Do away with more papers and link everything in a QR Code.

Centre storage

Go fully digital with QR Codes. Link all your important documents to unique QR Codes and have them stored in one QR Code where they can be accessed easily.

Sharing Information

Enjoy quick and seamless sharing of all types of documents with QR Codes. You can share PDFs, Audio and Images using QReate generating tool.

QReate provides both Dynamic and Static QR Codes.

The differences between both types of QR Codes is in the changes and updates possibilities.

Dynamic QR Codes

A dynamic QR Code is a QR Code that can be edited as many times as needed without having to reprint and redeploy the code. You can change little details in the information embeded, or change the URL completely.

Static QR Codes

A static QR Code is a QR Code that, once generated, cannot be overwritten. It can store information that is uneditable. It can be used where the information needs not to be changed, or for one-off campaigns.
